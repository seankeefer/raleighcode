PURPOSE AND INTENT. (ยง 8-2180)

This article sets forth the requirements for the Reuse Water System for the City
of Raleigh, hereafter referred to as the City. The Reuse Water System provides
an alternative water source for non-potable water demands. It is the
City&#8217;s policy to provide reuse water to the appropriate non-potable
demands to limit the growth in potable water. 
The objectives of this article are: 
1.	To reduce the potable water demand and extend the life of the existing raw
water supplies.
2.	To protect the environment by reducing discharges to the Neuse River.
3.	To help meet nitrogen reduction goals for the Neuse River.
This article shall apply to users of the City&#8217;s water and sewer system.
