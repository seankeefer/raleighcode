                                  CODE OF STATE

GENERAL MUNICIPAL POWERS. (ยง I-II-2.13)

The City Council shall continue under existing laws to have power to make and
provide for the execution of such ordinances for the City as the City Council
may deem proper not inconsistent with the laws of the land, and the City of
Raleigh shall have all the powers granted to municipalities by the general laws
of the State of North Carolina as the same may now or hereafter be enacted.
