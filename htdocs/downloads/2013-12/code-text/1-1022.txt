Special meetings of the Council may be called at any time by the Mayor, the
Mayor Pro Tem, or by any two (2) members of the Council, by causing a written
notice, setting the time, place and purpose of holding such meeting and signed
by the person or persons calling the meeting, to be served as provided in ยง2.4
of the Charter. The City Clerk shall prepare or cause to be prepared written
notices of all special meetings of the Council, upon appropriate request; and at
the time of making delivery of such notices to the members of the Council, a
copy of such notice of special meetings shall be delivered to representatives of
the news media in order that the citizens of the City may be advised of all such
special meetings.
