LABELING AND COLOR-CODING. (ยง 8-2190)

All reuse water valves, storage facilities and outlets shall be tagged or
labeled to warn the public or employees that the water is not intended for
drinking. 
All reuse water piping, valves, outlets and other appurtenances shall be
color-coded, taped, or otherwise marked to identify the source of the water as
being reuse water, as defined in the Public Utility Handbook.
