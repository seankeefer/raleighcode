                                  CODE OF STATE

SAFETY PERMIT. (ยง 12-2090)

The taxicab inspector shall have the authority to issue a taxicab safety permit
on forms prescribed by him for periods of time not to exceed ten (10) days. The
holder of such a permit shall be authorized to occupy the front seat of a
taxicab while in operation, and shall observe all rules and regulations of this
article while on duty.
