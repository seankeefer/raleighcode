DUTY TO REPORT DAMAGE. (ยง 8-2199)

Every person who observes damage to a manhole or to any other part of the reuse
water system, public or private, has a duty to report the damage to the Public
Utilities Department, and every permit holder whose reuse system is damaged
shall take immediate actions to stop or as best practical mitigate the flow of
the reuse water.
