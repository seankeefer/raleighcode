PEDESTRIAN CROSSING PROHIBITED. (ยง 11-2152)

It shall be unlawful for any person to cross a street, on foot or by any other
means, at a point and in a manner prohibited by this section and where a sign,
barricade or other notice indicates that such crossing is prohibited. Locations
where pedestrian crossing is so prohibited shall be as designated in Traffic
Schedule No. 12, &#8220;Pedestrian Crossing Prohibited.&#8221;
