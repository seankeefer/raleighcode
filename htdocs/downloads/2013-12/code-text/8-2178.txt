                                  CODE OF STATE

RALEIGH WATER SHORTAGE RESPONSE PLAN. (§ 8-2178)

The Raleigh Water Shortage Response Plan, as may be amended from time to time,
is hereby adopted by reference as fully as though set forth herein. A copy of
the latest copy of the Raleigh Water Shortage Response Plan is on file in the
City Clerk&#8217;s Office and in the Public Utilities Department. 
(Ord. No. 2010-793, §5, 10-19-10) 
Editor&#8217;s note— 
Ord. No. 2010-793, §5, adopted Oct. 19, 2010, repealed the former §8-2178, and
enacted a new §8-2178 as set out herein. The former §8-2178 pertained to golf
courses and derived from Ord. No. 2010-688, §4, adopted Jan. 19, 2010.
