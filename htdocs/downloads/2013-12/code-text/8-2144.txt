INSPECTION OF PROPERTY. (ยง 8-2144)

It shall be the duty, upon request of the Director of Public Utilities, of the
cross connection coordinator to cause inspections to be made of properties
served by the public water supply where cross-connections with the public water
supply are deemed possible. The frequency of inspections and reinspections shall
be set by the director of public utility.
