ANIMALS OR FOWL BANNED FROM SALE. (ยง 12-3006)

It shall be unlawful for any person to sell or offer for sale, or permit to be
sold or offered for sale, within the corporate limits of the City, baby
chickens, baby ducklings, or baby rabbits less than six (6) weeks of age;
provided, this section shall not apply to hatcheries raising chickens or ducks
expressly for the broiler market or for sale to farms.
