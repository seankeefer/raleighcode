NOTICE OF VIOLATION/REPEAT SAMPLING AND REPORTING. (ยง 8-2135)

(a) If sampling performed by a user indicates a violation, the user must notify
the POTW Director immediately and not less than within twenty-four (24) hours of
becoming aware of the violation. The user shall also repeat the sampling and
analysis and submit the results of the repeat analysis to the POTW Director
within thirty (30) days after becoming aware of the violation. If allowed by the
[POTW Director}, the user is not required to resample:

(i) if the POTW Director monitors at the user&#8217;s facility at least once a
month; or 
(ii)	if the POTW Director samples between the user&#8217;s initial sampling and
when the user receives the results of this sampling.
