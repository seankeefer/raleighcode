FUNCTIONS AND DUTIES OF THE DEPARTMENT. (ยง 5-2002)

(a) Inspection of fire safety devices and equipment, constructions and
obstructions;

(b) Enforcement of fire prevention codes; instruction in the correction of
violations and when necessary the correction of violations through the courts as

(c) Responding to all calls, fire or rescue and the suppression of all fires as
found;

(d) Training the City&#8217;s firefighting force in fire suppression, rescues

(e) Supervising the upkeep of all fire stations, engine companies, truck
companies, and auxiliary vehicles, including the testing and maintenance of fire
hydrants.
