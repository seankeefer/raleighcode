                                  CODE OF STATE

CONFINEMENT OF ANIMALS WITH HISTORY OF BITING PEOPLE. (ยง 12-3067)

All fierce, dangerous or vicious animals, including dogs that have a history of
unlawful biting of humans, shall be confined by the owner within a building or
secure enclosure or by the animal control warden in the animal control shelter.
Such animals shall not be released from confinement unless securely muzzled.
