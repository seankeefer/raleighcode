                                  CODE OF STATE

SAME—DRIVING IN FUNERAL OR OTHER PROCESSIONS. (§ 11-2131)

(a) A funeral composed of a procession of vehicles shall be identified by
burning lights on the cars in the procession while proceeding to the cemetery.

(b) Each driver in a funeral or other procession shall drive as near to the
right-hand edge of the roadway as practical and follow the vehicle ahead as
closely as practical and safe. 
(Code 1959 , §21-30)
