                                  CODE OF STATE

CLINGING TO MOVING VEHICLES. (ยง 11-2122)

(a) It shall be unlawful for any person riding upon any bicycle, motorcycle,
coaster, sled, roller skates or any toy vehicle to attach the same or himself to
any moving vehicle upon any roadway.

(b) It shall be unlawful for any person to ride the running board, fenders or
bumpers of any vehicle except on slow moving vehicles in a parade.
