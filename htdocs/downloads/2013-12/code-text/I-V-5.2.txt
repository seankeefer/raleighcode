                                  CODE OF STATE

CITY CLERK, CITY TREASURER AND CITY ATTORNEYAPPOINTMENT. (ยง I-V-5.2)

At the first meeting of the City Council after their election, or as soon
thereafter as practicable, the City Council shall appoint the following
officers, who shall each serve at the pleasure of the City Council and at a
compensation to be fixed by the Council, and who may be required to furnish bond
in such amount and with such surety as ordered by the City Council, in
accordance with the provisions of this act relating to the bonds of public
officials, and whose duties shall be as prescribed by this act and by the
general laws of North Carolina, a City Clerk and a City Treasurer, or in lieu
thereof, a City Clerk and Treasurer, and a City Attorney.
