FEE; SEPARATE LICENSE FOR EACH FACILITY OPERATED. (ยง 12-3042)

The annual license fee for a pet shop shall be imposed pursuant to the
provisions of Part 2, Chapter 2 of this Code relating to merchants. Pet shop
licensees shall be required to obtain a separate license for each other holding
facility operated on the same premises as the pet shop.
