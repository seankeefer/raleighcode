REMOVAL OF RESIDENCE. (ยง I-IV-4.3)

(a) In the case of removal of residence of any elective officer from the
territorial limits of the City, such removal shall, ipso facto, create a vacancy
in his office.

(b) In the case of removal of residence of any officer elected to represent a
specific electoral district from the electoral district for the representation
of which he was elected, such removal shall, ipso facto, create a vacancy in his
office.
