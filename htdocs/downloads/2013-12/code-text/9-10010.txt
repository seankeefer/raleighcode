FORM AND MEDIUM. (ยง 9-10010)

Any art acquired pursuant to this policy shall be designed and created by visual
artists. In addition to the traditional forms of art such as sculpture,
painting, fresco, glass, mosaic and others the public art requirement may also
be met by incorporating artistic elements into the overall architectural design
if created by a visual artist. Any art installed pursuant to this program shall
become and remain the property of the City of Raleigh.
