RIGHT TURNS ON RED TRAFFIC CONTROL SIGNAL LIGHTS PROHIBITED. (ยง 11-2105)

After authorized signs have been erected giving notice thereof, it shall be
unlawful to proceed to turn right while facing a red traffic light at those
locations specified in official Traffic Schedule No. 1, Electronic traffic
control signals.
