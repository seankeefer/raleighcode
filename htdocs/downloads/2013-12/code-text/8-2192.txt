STORAGE CONTAINERS AND TANK TRUCKS. (ยง 8-2192)

Storage containers, including tank trucks that come into contact with reuse
water, shall not be used to store or transport potable water that is used for
drinking or other potable purposes. The containers and trucks shall be clearly
labeled &#8220;REUSE WATER &#8211; DO NOT DRINK&#8221;. The containers shall not
be filled through on-board piping or removable hoses that may subsequently be
used to fill tanks with water from a potable water supply.
