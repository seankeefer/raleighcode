                                  CODE OF STATE

CITY MANAGER; APPOINTMENT. (ยง I-III-3.1)

The City Council shall appoint a City Manager, who shall be the administrative
head of the City government, and shall be responsible for the administration of
all departments. He shall be appointed with regard to merit only, and he need
not be a resident of the City when appointed. He shall hold office during the
pleasure of the City Council, and shall receive such compensation as the City
Council shall fix by ordinance.
