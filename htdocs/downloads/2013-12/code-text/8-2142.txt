COMPLIANCE WITH FEDERAL AND STATE LAW. (ยง 8-2142)

The City of Raleigh will comply with the Federal Safe Drinking Water Act, the
North Carolina Drinking Water Act, and North Carolina State Building Code, which
pertain to cross-connections, auxiliary intakes and interconnections, and
establish an effective ongoing program to control potential sources of
contamination of the public water supply.
