                                  CODE OF STATE

SILENCE CONSTITUTES AFFIRMATIVE VOTE. (§ 1-1032)

Unless a member of the Council is excused from voting in accordance with State
law, the member&#8217;s silence shall be recorded as an affirmative vote. 
Charter reference: Ordinance requirements, effective date, §§2.7, 2.8 
State law reference: Voting, G.S. 160A-75.
