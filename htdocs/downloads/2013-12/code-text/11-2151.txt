PEDESTRIAN STREETS. (ยง 11-2151)

(a) Exchange Street from Fayetteville Street to South Wilmington Street.

(b) Fayetteville Street, from its intersection with the southern boundary of
Morgan Street to its intersection with the northern boundary of Davie Street,
but excluding the intersections of Martin Street and Hargett Street. 
Cross reference: Pedestrian malls generally, Part 9, Chapter 7.
