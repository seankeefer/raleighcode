UNLAWFUL WATER USES. (ยง 8-2165)

It shall be unlawful for any for any person to use water in violation of this
Article or any of the mandatory water use restriction ordinances approved by the
Raleigh City Council. It shall also be unlawful for any person to use potable
water from the public water system, if reuse water from the City reuse water
supply system is equally available to the same location and the water use does
not require potable water quality.
