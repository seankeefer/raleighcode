PERMIT TO LOCATE, ERECT OR PLACE. (ยง 12-3032)

Before any person shall locate, erect or place any stable on any lot within the
corporate limits of the City, he shall make application to the Wake County
Health Department and the City Inspection Division. Such permits may be issued,
upon compliance with this chapter and with standards and procedures of those
agencies.
