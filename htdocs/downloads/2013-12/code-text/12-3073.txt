NOTICE OF AND APPEALS FROM DECISION OF AGENCIES. (ยง 12-3073)

Notice of recommendation not to issue a license under this chapter or to revoke
a license shall be given to the licensee. Administrative appeals shall be,
first, to the Director of Finance and then to the City Manager. An appeal of a
decision to recommend the revocation of a license shall suspend the operation of
that decision.
