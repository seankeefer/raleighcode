                                  CODE OF STATE

DEPARTMENTS ENUMERATED. (ยง I-VI-6.1)

(1) Department of Finance, which shall be headed by the City Accountant, who
shall also have the title of Director of Finance.

(2) Department of Public Safety, which shall be headed by the Director of Public
Safety.

(3) Department of Public Works, which shall be headed by the Director of Public
Works.

(4) Department of Planning, which shall be headed by the director of planning.

(5) Department of Public Utilities, which shall be headed by the Director of
Public Utilities.

(6) Department of Parks and Recreation, which shall be headed by the Director of
Parks and Recreation.

(7) Health Department and such other departments as may be created from time to
time by ordinance adopted by the City Council. Each of the departments and the
department heads shall be under the supervision of and directly responsible to
the City Manager. Department or division heads need not be resident[s] of the
City when appointed, except as herein specifically otherwise provided.
