                                  CODE OF STATE

DISCONNECTION AND RECONNECTION FEES. (§ II-2-3004)

The Bureau shall employ such staff as it deems necessary to carry out its duties
and responsibilities and in accord with its approved budget. Employees of the
Bureau shall not be considered as employees of the City for any purposes
whatsoever. 
(Ord. No. 1992-904, §6, 1-7-92)
