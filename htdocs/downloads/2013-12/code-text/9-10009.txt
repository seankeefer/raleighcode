PROJECT COORDINATION. (ยง 9-10009)

It is understood that coordination between the Public Art Program and City
construction management is vital to achieve timely completion of CIP projects.
In order to achieve this cooperation the chair of the ASP for a particular
project shall make every effort to expedite the public art side of a project and
the City construction management staff and arts staff shall cooperate fully with
the project ASP. The City shall include in any professional service RFP or RFQ
language that indicates one or more artists may be included on the design team
and will be selected by an independent process. The project architect shall be
informed during negotiations for the architect&#8217;s professional services of
the requirement for artist involvement in the process which may include the
artist on the project design team. Any unresolved conflict shall be decided
directly by the City Council.
