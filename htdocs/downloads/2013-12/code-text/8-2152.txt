                                  CODE OF STATE

CHANGE IN NATURE OF USE. (§ 8-2152)

The Public Utilities Department shall be notified by the consumer when the
nature of use of the property changes so as to change the hazard classification
of that property, as set forth in §§8-2147 through 8-2148 of this Code. 
(Ord. No. 1987-20, §1, 7-21-87)
