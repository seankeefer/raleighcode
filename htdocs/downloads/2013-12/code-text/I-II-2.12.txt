                                  CODE OF STATE

COMPENSATION AND ALLOWANCES PAID TO COUNCIL AND MAYOR. (§ I-II-2.12)

The compensation paid to the members of the City Council and the Mayor for their
services may be fixed, from time to time, by the Council as provided in G.S.
160A-64. 
(S.L. 1957, Ch. 341, §1; S.L. 1973, Ch. 319, §1(f))
