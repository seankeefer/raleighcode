PICKETING DEFINED. (ยง 12-1055)

The terms picket, pickets and picketing as used herein are deemed to include
&#8220;demonstrators,&#8221; persons participating in vigils and any action
primarily promoting or objecting to a policy upon those portions of the public
ways not used primarily for vehicular parking and moving traffic and not
constituting a parade.
