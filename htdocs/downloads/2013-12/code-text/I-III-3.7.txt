                                  CODE OF STATE

SALARIES. (ยง I-III-3.7)

Every officer, agent, employee and assistant of the City government (except the
Mayor and members of the City Council whose salaries are otherwise provided
for), shall receive such salary or compensation as the City Council shall by
budget appropriation or other appropriate action provide, payable in equal
semi-monthly installments, unless the City Council shall order payments to be
made at other intervals.
