                                  CODE OF STATE

MINUTES—KEEPING, RECORDING, PRESENTING FOR APPROVAL, CORRECTION. (§ 1-1027)

Minutes of all Council meetings shall be kept and recorded by the City Clerk in
a permanent minute book. They shall be presented to the Council for approval.
Correction of minutes of preceding meetings may be made only by a majority vote
of the members of the Council present. 
(Code 1959 , §2-7)
