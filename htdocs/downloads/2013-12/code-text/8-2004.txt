RESPONSIBILITY FOR MAKING CONNECTIONS. (§ 8-2004)

All water and sewer services shall be installed, extended, replaced or repaired
by City forces from the City&#8217;s main to the property line unless otherwise
permitted by the Director of Public Utilities; provided, no such permission
shall be granted for work within the corporate limits in public street
rights-of-way except by licensed utility contractors. All water and sewer
connections in new developments shall be the responsibility of the developer and
at his expense in accordance with City standards and specifications. 
(Code 1959 , §23-25; Ord. No. 1985-543, §3, 4-16-85; Ord. No. 1995-760-TC-122,
§14, TC-20C-95, 11-21-95) 
Cross references: Street maintenance, Part 7, Ch. 2; technical provisions
concerning plumbing requirement on making utility connections, §10-6071 et seq.

State law references: Permits required for work in state roads, G.S. 136-93;
license for public utility contractors, G.S. 87-10.
