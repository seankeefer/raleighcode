                                  CODE OF STATE

ADOPTION OF RULES OF PROCEDURE; MEETINGS OPEN TO PUBLIC. (ยง I-II-2.6)

The City Council may from time to time establish rules for its proceedings as
may be deemed necessary and proper. All legislative sessions of the City Council
shall be open to the public, and the City Council shall not by executive session
or otherwise vote on any question in private session. A full and accurate
journal of the proceedings shall be kept and shall be open to the inspection of
any qualified registered voter of the City.
