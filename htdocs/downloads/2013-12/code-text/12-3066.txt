SUMMARY DESTRUCTION OF ANIMALS FOR HUMANE REASONS. (ยง 12-3066)

When, in the judgment of the person in charge of impoundment facility, it is
determined that any impounded animal should be destroyed for humane reasons, or
to protect the public from imminent danger to persons or property, such animal
may be destroyed without regard to any time limitations otherwise established
herein.
