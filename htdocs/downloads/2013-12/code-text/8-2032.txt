ASSESSMENT; CITY TO SHARE COST OF OVERSIZED LINES. (ยง 8-2032)

In the event it is necessary or desirable in the opinion of the Director of
Public Utilities to lay a water main larger than a six-inch main, or sewer main
larger than an eight-inch main, the City will bear the cost of the difference
between the size of main required and a six-inch water main or eight-inch sewer
main.
