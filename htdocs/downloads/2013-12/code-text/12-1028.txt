                                  CODE OF STATE

TREES, PLANTING, INJURING, DESTROYING. (ยง 12-1028)

Citizens may plant trees in front of their lots and around their lots, on the
sidewalks, under the supervision of the director of parks. No tree shall be
planted in the streets or gutters. No person shall cut, destroy or injure any
tree or box around same without permission from the director of parks.
