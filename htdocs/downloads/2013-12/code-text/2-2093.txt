POWERS AND DUTIES. (ยง 2-2093)

The Bureau is to promote travel to and tourism in the City and County. To
fulfill its duties, the Bureau may contract with any person, to advise and
assist it in the promotion of travel, tourism and conventions. The Bureau may
only spend funds for visitor promotion and convention promotion.
