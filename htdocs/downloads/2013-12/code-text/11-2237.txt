FAILURE TO REMOVE SIGNS. (ยง 11-2237)

It shall be a misdemeanor for any person, persons or corporation to leave signs,
signals, and/or warning devices standing in the street or right-of-way when the
obstruction for which such signs, signals and/or warning devices were erected
has been removed.
