LIMITATION OF LIABILITY. (ยง 8-2156)

The City shall not be held liable, for any cause, for failure to detect any unit
failing to operate adequately, or failure to identify any specific hazard, which
may result in contamination of its public water supply, nor shall this article
diminish the responsibility of any property owner from whose property a
contamination of the public water supply may originate.
