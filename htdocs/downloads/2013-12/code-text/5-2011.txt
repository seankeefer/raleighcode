INTERFERENCE WITH FIREFIGHTER; GETTING ON OR OFF APPARATUS; LOITERING; MEDDLING
WITH APPARATUS. (ยง 5-2011)

It shall be unlawful for any person to interfere with a firefighter in the
discharge of his duty, or to get on or off of the apparatus going to or from a
fire without authority from the Chief or Assistant Chief, or to loiter about the
engine houses, or for any person to meddle with the engine, hose, or apparatus.
