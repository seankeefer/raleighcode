                                  CODE OF STATE

REPORTS OF CHANGED CONDITIONS. (§ 8-2132)

(1) The POTW Director may require the user to submit such information as may be
deemed necessary to evaluate the changed condition, including the submission of
a waste water discharge permit application under §8-2127 of this article.

(2) The POTW Director may issue a waste water discharge permit under §8-2127 of
this article or modify an existing waste water discharge permit under §8-2127
of this article in response to changed conditions or anticipated changed
conditions.

(3) For purposes of this requirement, significant changes include, but are not
limited to, flow or pollutant increases of twenty (20) per cent or greater, and
the discharge of any previously unreported pollutants. 
(Ord. No. 2011-981, §1, 12-6-11)
