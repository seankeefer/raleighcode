                                  CODE OF STATE

TURNING AT OR BETWEEN INTERSECTIONS. (ยง 11-2104)

After authorized signs have been erected giving notice thereof, it shall be
unlawful to make a left turn, right turn, or a straight-through movement,
respectively, at the locations set out in official Traffic Schedule No. 8,
Restricted turns at or between intersections.
