REPLACEMENT OF REUSE WATER SERVICE STUBS. (ยง 8-2202)

In the event of the paving or widening of streets, or the raising or lowering of
the grade of a street, or in the installation of curbs or gutters and/or
sidewalks, or in the case of the installation of new reuse water lines initiated
by the public, all reuse water service stubs may be replaced so as to meet the
standards prescribed by the City plumbing code. There shall be no charge for
such replacements.
