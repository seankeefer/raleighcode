                                  CODE OF STATE

UNLOADING COAL ON SIDEWALKS. (ยง 12-1029)

Every manhole or other aperture located within the sidewalk areas of the City
and used for delivering coal into the basement of an adjacent building shall be
located within two (2) feet of the curb line of the street. Coal shall not be
permitted to be shoveled or thrown across the sidewalk.
