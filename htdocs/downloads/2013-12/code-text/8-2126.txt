WASTE WATER DISCHARGERS. (ยง 8-2126)

It shall be unlawful for any person to connect or discharge to the POTW without
first obtaining the permission of the City. When requested by the POTW Director,
a user must submit information on the nature and characteristics of its waste
water within thirty (30) days of the request. The POTW Director is authorized to
prepare a form for this purpose and may periodically require users to update
this information.
