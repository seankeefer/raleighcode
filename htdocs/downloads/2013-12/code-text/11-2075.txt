                                  CODE OF STATE

EMERGING FROM ALLEY OR PRIVATE DRIVEWAY. (ยง 11-2075)

The driver of a vehicle emerging from an alley, driveway, or building shall stop
that vehicle immediately prior to driving on to a sidewalk or into a sidewalk
area across any alleyway, and upon entering the roadway shall yield the
right-of-way to all vehicles approaching on the roadway.
