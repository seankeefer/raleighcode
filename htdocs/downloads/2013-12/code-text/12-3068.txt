DISPOSAL OF ANIMAL CARCASSES. (ยง 12-3068)

The Solid Waste Services Director, upon request of any person, or otherwise, may
pick up and dispose of any animal carcass within the area of his jurisdiction.
When the owner of an animal, the carcass of which is disposed of by a public
agency, can be identified, he shall be billed for the cost of the disposition.
